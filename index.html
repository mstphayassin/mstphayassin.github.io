I am a Master's student in Civil Engineering with an interest in hydrotechnical problems and geo-hazards. My research focuses on estimating the breach characteristics of failures of embankment dams, with an emphasis on small dams. My work deals with both empirical equations and physically-based numerical models for dam failure simulations.
